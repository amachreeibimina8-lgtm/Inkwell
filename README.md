# Inkwell

Inkwell is an interactive, all-in-one digital workspace and creative suite designed for authors and novelists. It features real-time manuscript analysis, design tools, and creative inspiration boards to help writers go from a blank page to a finished book.

## ✨ Key Features

* **📜 Manuscript Analyzer:** Upload a PDF chapter or excerpt to extract text, analyze pacing and style, and receive an instant structural score out of 10.
* **🖋️ Genre Sparks:** Generate short, original mood pieces tailored to specific genres to break writer's block and lock into a narrative voice.
* **📕 Cover Gallery:** Browse and personalize ready-made photographic, illustrated, and typographic book covers, fully customizable for self-publishing or platforms like Wattpad.
* **🌍 Visual Inspiration:** Explore a dynamic visual board of reference images categorized to spark new ideas for characters and settings.
* **✨ Ask AI:** A free-form creative assistant built to instantly generate scenes, character names, plot twists, blurbs, or dialogue on demand.
* **📚 Shared Library:** A collaborative community hub where writers can publish their stories and browse work shared by other authors.
* **👤 Author Profile:** Customize your author bio and maintain a personal gallery of uploaded reference photos for ongoing inspiration.

## 🛠️ How It Works
Inkwell runs a live build featuring real-time editing feedback and scores generated on the fly. 

1. **Upload:** Drop in your novel, chapter, or excerpt via PDF.
2. **Refine:** Edit the extracted text directly inside the browser.
3. **Analyze:** Click **"Analyze & rate"** to trigger live editor's notes and structural feedback in the margin.
